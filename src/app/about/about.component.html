Wafi Lounge Restaurant, based on the ground floor, is a contemporary Lebanese restaurant on Clayton Road West that serves authentic Lebanese cuisine.

Allow your tastebuds to experience what fresh, authentic meze tastes like. Our team of expert chefs have put together a very special menu for you to experience. The Wafi Lounge cocktail bar on the first floor serves Oasis and Wafi Restaurant.

An elegant lounge in the style of its drink and service, offering a wide range of speciality cocktails, mocktails, beers, wines and champagnes along with spirits.
